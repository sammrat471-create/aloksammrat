About Me

I’m Alok Kumar, known online as AlokSammrat983 — a 17-year-old entrepreneur, trader, and student who believes that age is never a limit to achieving big dreams. At a remarkably young age, I founded my own brand LuxeLayer, which operates under Sammrat GROUP PVT. LTD. This venture, valued at over ₹10 crore, reflects my passion for innovation, business, and creating meaningful impact.

From the very beginning, my goal has never been just financial success — it has always been about making a difference. Through LuxeLayer and other initiatives, I strive to combine creativity, entrepreneurship, and purpose to inspire others and drive positive change.

My mission is to support the underprivileged and create opportunities that improve lives. A significant part of my company’s revenue is donated to healthcare, helping save lives and provide medical aid, and to education, ensuring that every child has access to learning and a brighter future. True success is measured not just by wealth, but by the ability to uplift others, spread knowledge, and leave a lasting impact on society.

At every step of my journey, my vision remains clear — to grow as a learner and a leader, inspire youth, and continue building ventures that make a real difference in the world.
